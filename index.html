Hola! my name is Enrique Contreras Alba but everyone calls me Kike

I’m a UX/UI Designer with over four years conceptualizing and crafting user-centric digital solutions.

Luckily, I’ve always had the opportunity to be out in the world, to experience other cultures and be surrounded by people that enrich my imagination and the way I approach creativity in design.

With an innate ability to apply color, I’m fascinated by bringing beauty and functionality together, creating digital products that improve people’s lives. Ver la vida de color de rosa, as we say here in Spain.

So, let’s connect and let me show you the power of what UX/UI design can do for you!
